November 2021 - Guangdong NSF Project Successfully Got Approved

A General Research Fund project of Natural Science Foundation of Guangdong (广东省基础与应用基础研究基金自然科学基金面上项目) was successfully approved. The project money is CNY 100,000.00, and the duration is from Jan. 1, 2022 to Dec. 31, 2024. Dr. Yuxiang Sun is the principal investigator of this project. The success rate is 24.96%.

January 2021- Most Cited Articles in Robotics and Autonomous Systems (RAS)

December 2020 - Popular Articles in IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters (RA-L)

This work was finished by Dr. Yuxiang Sun during his postdoctoral studies. The paper title is: RTFNet: RGB-Thermal Fusion Network for Semantic Segmentation of Urban Scenes. The code is available at: https://github.com/yuxiangsun/RTFNet.
